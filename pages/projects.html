Twitchue

Twitchue is an open-source Node.js Electron app for custom Philips Hue lighting effects in response to live-streaming events on Twitch. Whenever you get a new subscriber, follower, or donation you can create live lighting effects to let provide even more visual feedback on your stream. Feel free to check it out over at https://github.com/briliantbrandon/twitchue.
